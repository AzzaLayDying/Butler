Note on Marriage

James, son of Joseph & Charlotte was born in Birmingham England circa.1827. Dinah was baptised at Aston Cantlow, England 31 st July 1831. 9no further details) Married in Birmingham c. 1850. From documents found they arrived in NZ with their 2 sons in 1858. Five more sons were born, making a family of 7 sons.

James was a bricklayer and a lay preacher in the Methodist Church. No family circumstances are known. He died 1st June 1905 at Waipukurau aged 78 yrs. Dinah died in Carterton 1st Jan 1885 and is buried at Taratahi near Masterton, she was 53 yrs old.

Headstone Reads:

In fond rememberance of Dinah Cornwall who dies Jan 1st 1885 age 54 yrs.

The love may pass away from earth as stars fall from the skies.

But in our hearts who loved her most the memory never dies.

Though thou has called me resign what most I prize, never was mine. I only yield thee what was thine.

Thy will be done.
