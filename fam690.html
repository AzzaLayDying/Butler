Note on Husband: Pastor Robert Kells I

Ordained a clergyman in the Established Church. He taught school and did pastoral work in Scotland. He received a commission to become Warden of the Parish of Templeport, Co Cavan,Ireland. He received a grant or the overcharge of a large tract of land on the townlands of Bufflen,Umaugh & Kellenaff in Co Cavan near Ballyconnell. He resided in Buffalen and the property was distributed amongst his relatives and tenants. A considerable amount is still owned by his or their descendants.
