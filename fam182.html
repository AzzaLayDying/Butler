Note on Husband: Henry KELLS (2)

Henry and his wife Sarah and son William Henry arrived in NZ at Port Nicholson, Wellington on the 18th of December 1852

 

Henry Kells (1824 – 1872) joined the 37th Regiment of Foot on the 14th September 1843 (Regimental Number 2745).

 

He married Sarah Hume (1828 - 1894) on the 10th September 1847 at Seapatrick Parish (Anglican) Church (outside Banbridge), County Down, Ireland; her father was recorded in the marriage certificate as William Hume.

 

Henry transferred to the 65th Regiment of Foot, and to join his Regiment he sailed for New Zealand on the “True Briton” arriving at Port Nicholson, Wellington, on the 18th December 1852. On the 31st December 1853 Corporal Kells purchased his discharge from the 65th Regiment in Wellington. - Trevor McLean 13/9/17

 

He became a Hotel Keeper in Wellington; his first hotel, The Nelson was on Lambton Quay and he was proprietor of the Surprise Hotel in Karori at the time of his death in July 1872. References: https://www.wikitree.com/wiki/Kells-16
