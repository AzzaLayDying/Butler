Note on Wife: Sarah Evelina Wilkin (nee TUCKER)

Biography

Sarah Evelina Tucker was born on 2 December 1832 to Ruth Seon Outerbridge, age 35, and Samuel Thomas Tucker, age 35.

 

Sarah Evelina Tucker married William Henry Wilkin on 27 February 1851 when she was 18 years old. They had eight children in 17 years.

 

Sarah Evelina Tucker died on 26 December 1904 in Dagenham, Saint Anthony, Montserrat, when she was 72 years old.
