Charles & Elizabeth were married on 28 dec 1830 at Prestbury Church, Prestbury Cheshire, England

Charles is buried next to his son Randle, in an unmarked grave.

Randal is buried next to (Mary Crompton 1839-1869 believed to be the first burial in the Churchyard..) The grave with the wooden surround grave, in the photo.

Note on Marriage 2

Charles and Elizabeth came to New Zealand on the Sailing Ship "Canterbury" and arrived at Lyttelton on 31st October 1851, bringing with them their remaining two children, Matthew aged 6 yrs and Edwin aged 1 year. These two children were the sole survivors of the first ten children. The others having died in England. On three occasions the family were left childless.

 

They were among the first Settlers to take up land at Governors Bay, " Ohinetahi" being the Maori name, in Canterbury New Zealand.

 

Charles was shown on the NZ Electoral Roll of 1853 as being a Quarryman of Governors Bay..
