Note on Husband: Henry James HICKTON

Henry is shown in records to have lived in Paihea in 1878-1879. He was an accountant and worked in the gum store at Kaikohe and is listed in the Auckland Directory for 1894 & 1896 as a horse trainer, Avondale.

Note on Wife: Alice Elizabeth WILLIAMSON

Alice, tilthe time of her death, wore a coat which had belonged to Cpt Frederick Barker. It was very heavy and warm, lined with astrakan and had very fascinating buttons. Alice set much store by this coat.
