Note on Wife: Ina Aileen Gall (nee DUKE)

LifeSketch: Aileen was the daughter of Mansergh Pace and Emily DUKE. She worked in the Royal Canadian Bank in Antigua until she married Herbert Frederick Douglas GALL. They had two daughters- Cynthia and Claire (m. O'Carroll).
