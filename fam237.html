Note on Husband: DAVID Henry GODWIN

US Census Date 1851: Living with his parents and 2 young sisters Ann & Harriet in Crompton RD, Macclesfield, England, Aged 14.

Occupation: Broad Silk Weaver.

He joined the military and was attached to the 65th Regiment (1854) Weymouth and Jersey, Henry emigrated from England to NZ where he remained with the 65th regiment.

In 1865 the 65th disbanded and a lot of its members returned to England. David stayed and married Charlottle Theresa Stillwell in 1871. They had at least 6 children.

Biography

Henry was born about 1835. He was the son of Edward Godwin and Susanna Fitton.

 

Christening Date: 2 October 1836

Event Place: Macclesfield, Cheshire, England[1]

 

 

 

David passed away in 1896 at the age of 61.

Note on Wife: Charlotte Teresa GODWIN WINDSOR (nee STILLWELL)

Charlotte was born in 1852, the daughter of John Stillwell and Mary New.

She married David "Henry" Godwin in 1871. They had at least 8 children. Frances, Frank, Martha, Joseph, Henry, Rose, Alfred and Harriet.

Her husband David passed away in 1896.

Charlotte remarried to John Joseph Windsor in 1900.

She passed away in 1908 at the age of 56.
