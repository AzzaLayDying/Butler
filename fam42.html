Note on Marriage

Frank married Beatrice at the home of her Uncle Henry Hicton on Rosebank Rd, Avondale, Auckland. Frank aged 27 was a bricklayer and Beatrice aged 20 was a domestic. Frank had a variety of jobs in his working life:bricklaying,glazier,cabinet maker,butcher. After Beatrices death Frank lived with his son Greg in Otahuhu. He is buried with his mother Sophia and 2 of her sons at Purewa Cemetery, Meadowbank,Auckland. Beatrce is buried with her mother at Waikaraka Cemetery Onehunga,Auckland

Last residence Astley Ave, New Lynn.
