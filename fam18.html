Note on Husband: DESMOND Allen WHITE (1)

Choir and Piano

Des sung in Uncle Toms choir for some years. He also learnt the piano for three years or so.

Note on Husband: DESMOND Allen WHITE (2)

Cycling

Des loved his cycling. He joined the Manukau Amateur Cycling Club in 1941 and had a life long association with his club. He was successful at both Junior and Senior levels. Later in his sporting career he established and organised the Thames 6 Day Road Race. As children, we remember travelling as a family with Dad so that he could watch various road races around the country. Thames in particular was a common venue.

Note on Husband: DESMOND Allen WHITE (3)

Love of Speedway

Des had a life long love of speedway. Along with some of his cycling mates they regularly 'worked' at western springs as 'pushers' to get the midget racing cars started. Once married, with children, a visit to speedway was a regular Saturday night out for the family.
