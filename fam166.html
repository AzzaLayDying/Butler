Note on Wife: Kathleen (Cass) Wood (nee MCCURDY)

Kathleen Wood (born McCurdy) was born on February 8 1896, in Thames, Hauraki, New Zealand, to John jnr McCurdy and Harriet Annie McCurdy (born Godwin).

John was born circa 1860, in Edinburgh, Scotland.

Harriet was born on May 14 1872, in Thames, Shortland, Coromandel, New Zealand.

Kathleen had 5 siblings: Rose Ann McCurdy, John Jack McCurdy, Alfred McCurdy, Gladys Eileen McCurdy (born Godwin) and Edith Rose McCurdy.

Kathleen married Frederick Wood on January 31 1918, at age 21 in Toowoomba, Queensland, Australia.

Frederick was born on February 15 1884, in Stourbridge, Worchester, West Midlands, England.

They had 2 daughters: Doreen Mavis Warbrooke (born Wood) and Maire Francis WOOD.

Kathleen passed away on May 25 1987, at age 91 in Thames, Hauraki, New Zealand.
