  • Emigration:

  • Oct 31, 1851 (age 43)

  • from England to Lyttleton, New Zealand

  •  

  • They had ten children in Cheshire England before arriving in Lyttelton New Zealand on 31 Oct 1851. Out of those ten only Matthew & Randel survived to leave with them on their journey on the ship "Canterbury" taking 136 days. Charles and Elizabeth settled in Govenors Bay, Canterbury where they had two more sons Charles & (Joseph my Gt Grandfather)

    Charles and Isabella's eight children that died in England are listed below.
