Note on Marriage

Matthew & Isabella had twelve children out of those twelve three died in infancy :

 

"Matthew" born 27th June 1883 and died same day at Little River Canterbury.

"Annie" born 27th Jan 1887 Beaconsfield Feilding, died 12 days later on 9th Feb 1887.

"Isabella" born 22nd Dec 1887 Beaconsfield Feilding, died 14 days later on 5th Feb 1888.

Annie & Isabella are buried on the farm at Beaconsfield.

 

The site was marked by the planting of a walnut tree.. And in later years by a memorial plaque organised by Aubrey Macdonald Fitton 1916-2005, Grandson of Matthew & Isabella Fitton, with donations from the family..
